 Prompts for Students

1) Understand anything fast
Explain [topic] in the simplest way possible with a real life example. Then give me a slightly deeper explanation so I understand the full picture.

2)Summarize long stuff
Summarize this text into short points , highlight the key takeaways in bold , and tell me what I should remember long term

3)Solve problems step by step
Break down how to solve [problem] in clear steps, explain why each step matters, and warn me about common mistakes beginners make.

4)Learn anything in 10 minutes
Teach me the basics of [topic] in under 10 mins, using examples and simple analogies. End with 3 questions to check if I understood it.

5)Doing research first
Give me a clean, factual, overview of [topic] with verified points. Then list sources I can trust and mention 1 insight most people don't know.

6)Make better decisions
Give me the pros,cons and best option for [decision],based on my short context [add context]. Also suggest what I should avoid if I choose this option.
